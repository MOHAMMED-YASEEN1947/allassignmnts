spaghetti recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and garnish with Parmesan cheese.

Chocolate cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch round pans. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar, and cocoa in a bowl. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry ingredients and mix well. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour the batter into the pans and bake for 30-35 minutes. Let the cakes cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans to cool completely.
